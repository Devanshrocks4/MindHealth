About Our Platform

To develop a PHP-based platform for individuals to self-assess their mental health status and receive actionable guidance for support. The system will use validated mental health screening tools to evaluate responses and provide users with a clear interpretation of their mental health status. It will also offer recommendations and resources for seeking professional help if needed.

Our Mission

We aim to make mental health assessment accessible and stigma-free. Our platform provides validated tools like PHQ-9 and GAD-7 to help individuals understand their mental health status and connect with appropriate resources.

How It Works

  • • Take validated mental health assessments
  • • Receive instant results and interpretation
  • • Get personalized resource recommendations
  • • Download your results for future reference
